Mission

The cyber threat
is real. The solution
is education.

Most cyberattacks succeed not because of sophisticated technology — but because people weren't given the tools to recognize them. Cyber for Everyone exists to change that.

Free training materials for individuals and communities
Plain-language guides — no jargon, no prerequisites
Workshops, resources, and partnerships at scale
Built by security professionals, for everyone else

Curriculum

What we teach

Six foundational topics every person should know — free, always.

01

Phishing Awareness

How to spot deceptive emails, texts, and websites trying to steal your credentials or personal data.

02

Passwords & MFA

Building strong, unique passwords and enabling multi-factor authentication across your accounts.

03

Online Scams

Recognizing common fraud schemes — from romance scams to fake tech support calls and IRS impersonators.

04

Device Safety

Keeping your phone, computer, and home network secure through updates, settings, and safe habits.

05

Privacy Basics

Understanding what data apps and websites collect — and how to limit exposure without giving up functionality.

06

Social Engineering

How attackers manipulate people through urgency, authority, and trust — and how to stop them cold.
